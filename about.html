ABOUT US  

Nine Seas Group of Companies is established in 2002 by investing in Agriculture and Agro based industries in Myanmar.

With a dedicated passion and expertise, we specialize in manufacturing, processing, trading and exporting of all kinds of Myanmar's famous rice which produced by the synergy of goodhearted Myanmar farmers.

The company now processes the state of the art building of Rice Reprocessing Plant in Yangon and other integrated Rice Mills in different strategic areas. Moreover we also professionally engage in the contract farming, sponsoring researching of rice seeds, dealing with government body for international export and supplying agricultural machinery.

While Myanmar is reborn as Rice Bowl of Asia, we are proud to say that we have become one of the leading rice merchants in the country.
