Nine Seas Manufacturing Co.,Ltd

Nine Seas Co.,Ltd known for its long years of remarkable performance and has stood amidst trustful and dedicated service to supply quality goods to its market. Supplying rice to various international companies and government institution. Specious warehouse combined with 500 Tons Milling factory that operated by well trained and skilled employees. Well supervised with a strict quality control and inspection at every stage ensuring its products of a high standard.

We have total capacity of production of fine rice 700 Metric Tons per day from 3 Rice Mills. Ton 500 Rice Reprocessing Plant is located in East Dagon, Yangon and all machinery equipped in the plant is world leading brand “BUHLER” which made in Germany. It was built in 2010 and completed in 2011. It was commissioned by German engineers.

Then, Ton 100 Rice Mills are located in (1) Kyauk Tagar, Bago Division and (2) Pathein, Ayeyarwaddy Division where are naturally fertile and have a long history of rice production.
